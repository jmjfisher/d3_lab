Olympic Medal Performance

An application to visualize medal performance at various Olympic games with respect to GDP.
Standard Index was derived by normalizing a country's medal count by $1000 of GDP per person.
3:2:1 Index augments only the medal count, where a gold is worth 3, a silver 2, and a bronze 1.
Use the drop down to change the Olympics or index type and click to pan or scroll to zoom in the map.
